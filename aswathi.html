Profile

Kalamandalam Aswathi was born on 09th July 1996. She has studied Mohiniattam at Kerala Kalamandalam Deemed University under Kalamandalam Leelamma (Late), Kalamandalam Hymavathy, Kalamandalam Sujatha, Prof. Lakshmi Ramaswami, and Sree latha Vinod.

She has been working as a dance teacher in Kathakali School, Cheruthuruthy since 2014. She has participated in Kerala Tourism Partnership programs in the years 2015 and 2016 in major cities of India. In 2015, she had aprticipated in National Games which was held at Trivandrum, Kerala. She has also participated in IFFK 2013 at Trivandrum. Had participated in a dance performance in Singapore in the year 2015. Had performed in Mohinyattam programmes visualizing the poems: "Kerala Ganam" by Mahakavi P. Kunjiraman Nair, "Amma" by O.N.V. Kurup and "Karukare Karmukil" by Kavalam Narayana Panicker. SHe has also performed in "Galaxy of Musician", a dance depiction of Raja Ravi Varma's painting. Choreography for Jackfruit Tree's Life, based on a painting by Rajan Krishnan in 2018.

Awards:

Kalamandalam Kshemavathi Endowment and Kalamandalam Hymavathi Endowment both of which were awarded by Kerala Kalamandalam Deemed University.

Mohiniyattam

Mohiniyattam is a semi-classical dance form of Kerala. It is perfomed by women. The word Mohini means a maiden who steals the heart of the onlooker. It is thought that Vaishnava devotees gave the name of Mohiniattam to this dance form. Legend says that Lord Vishnu took on the guise of a Mohini, the arch enchantress, to enthrall people. The movements are graceful and the costumes are sober and attractive.

Bharathanatyam

Bharatha Natyam is one of the oldest form of dance. This form of dance is based on the ancient text on dance. Bharatha stands for Bhava(mood), Raga(music), and Tala(rhythm), while Natyam stands for Nritta. Movement, mime and music are given equal importance in Bharatha Natyam. A Bharatha Natyam performance is rendered by a solo artist, or a duo, or as a group. Bharatha Natyam is poetry in motion.
